Our Mission
Amplon's Independent Voice

Crystal News Network is the Republic of Amplon's most trusted independent news organisation. Founded to bring clarity to a complex world, CNN delivers breaking stories, in-depth reports, and live coverage from across Amplon and beyond.

Our reporters are embedded throughout the Republic, providing real-time coverage of the events that shape our nation — from the halls of government to the streets of every district. We believe every story deserves to be told, and told well.

Submissions
Report a Story

Crystal News Network is a community-driven network open to all citizens of Amplon. Anyone can file a report — provide your name, a headline, a category, and your story. An image is optional but encouraged. We publish all community submissions in real time.
